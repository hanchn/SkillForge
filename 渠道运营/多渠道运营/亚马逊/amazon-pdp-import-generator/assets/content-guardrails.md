# Amazon Content Guardrails

Before generating copy, record the source for brand, model, material, dimensions, included components, use cases, compatibility, care, warranty, testing, certifications, safety, origin, and variation differences.

Remove or qualify any claim without evidence. Keep search terms relevant and avoid competitor brands, subjective claims presented as facts, repetition, promotional language that violates the target field, and claims prohibited by the current category or marketplace.

Do not use high-volume or trending terms unless they are materially relevant to the verified product. Do not represent BSR as query rank, sponsored placement as organic placement, or a single current observation as a multi-month trend. SEO/GEO coverage must improve factual discoverability and semantic clarity without inventing attributes, comparisons, reviews, Q&A, endorsements, scarcity, certifications, safety, health, environmental, or performance claims.
