# Acceptance

- [ ] Entity, market, audience questions, and owned canonical source are explicit.
- [ ] Every material claim is supported, narrowed, qualified, or removed.
- [ ] Answer passages contain subject, answer, evidence, conditions, and limits.
- [ ] Statistics and comparisons retain date, geography, sample, and methodology where relevant.
- [ ] Structured data is recommended only for visible eligible content.
- [ ] Distribution avoids spam, fake consensus, planted endorsements, and manipulation.
- [ ] Measurement samples answer accuracy and citation behavior without promising deterministic rank.
