# Citation Readiness

A citation-ready passage has a clear subject, direct answer, bounded claim, nearby evidence, date or version when relevant, and limitations. It does not depend on a slogan or unexplained pronoun.

Audit entity consistency, claim provenance, primary-source availability, author accountability, update date, extractable tables, meaningful headings, canonical page ownership, indexability, structured-data eligibility, and conflicts across public pages.

High-risk claims include health, safety, legal, financial, environmental, comparative superiority, performance guarantees, certifications, customer counts, and market leadership. Require exact evidence and scope.
