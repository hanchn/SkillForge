# Acceptance

- [ ] Market, language, audience, page type, intent, and conversion goal are explicit.
- [ ] Keyword claims distinguish measured data from hypotheses.
- [ ] Product, policy, price, review, and authority claims are traceable.
- [ ] One primary page job is clear and cannibalization risk is addressed.
- [ ] Copy answers the search task and adds useful information instead of repeating terms.
- [ ] Metadata, headings, internal links, images, and schema are page-appropriate.
- [ ] Technical blockers and content recommendations are separated.
- [ ] Measurement includes qualified business outcomes, not rankings alone.
- [ ] No ranking guarantee, keyword stuffing, doorway pattern, or invented fact appears.

- [ ] `seo_audit_or_content.md` 已完整交付，并有下游使用者、验收证据和剩余风险
- [ ] `keyword_intent_map.md` 已完整交付，并有下游使用者、验收证据和剩余风险
- [ ] `measurement_plan.md` 已完整交付，并有下游使用者、验收证据和剩余风险
- [ ] 已覆盖正常路径、关键异常、暂停条件、人工升级、补偿或回滚
- [ ] 最终结论明确事实、假设、未知项、owner、审批和下一次复核时间
