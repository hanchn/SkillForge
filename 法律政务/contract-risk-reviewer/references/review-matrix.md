# Contract Review Matrix

Review every topic for clause presence, internal consistency, obligation, remedy, operational owner, and jurisdiction-specific escalation.

## Commercial core

Parties and authority; scope; deliverables; milestones; acceptance; price; taxes; invoicing; expenses; credits; renewal; suspension; termination; transition.

## Risk allocation

Representations; warranties; disclaimers; indemnity trigger, scope, procedure, and control; direct and indirect damages; liability cap, carve-outs, aggregation, and super-cap; insurance.

## Assets and information

Pre-existing and developed IP; license scope; feedback; open source; confidentiality definition, exclusions, use, disclosure, security, return, deletion, and survival; privacy roles; subprocessors; breach notice; data location.

## Governance

Compliance; audit; records; subcontracting; assignment; change control; force majeure; notices; order of precedence; amendment; severability; waiver; governing law; venue; arbitration; language.

## Severity

- Critical: likely deal blocker, uncapped or existential exposure, unlawful or unperformable duty
- High: material financial, legal, security, IP, or operational exposure
- Medium: meaningful ambiguity or imbalance that should be negotiated or controlled
- Low: drafting, clarity, or administrative improvement
