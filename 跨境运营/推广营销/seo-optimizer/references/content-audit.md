# SEO Content Audit

Check: target market and language; page purpose; verified facts; search intent; information gain; page uniqueness; title and H1 alignment; heading logic; answer placement; supporting evidence; images and alt purpose; internal links; canonical and indexability; status code; renderability; mobile experience; localization; structured-data eligibility; conversion path; analytics measurement.

Classify findings as content, technical, architecture, authority, conversion, or measurement. State evidence, impact, confidence, owner, and acceptance criterion.
