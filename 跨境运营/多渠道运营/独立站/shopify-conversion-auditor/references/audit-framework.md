# Shopify Conversion Audit Framework

## Journey questions

- Landing: Am I in the right place and is the promise matched to the ad or query?
- Collection: Can I narrow choices and compare meaningful differences?
- Product: Is this right for me, can I trust it, and what will it cost and when arrive?
- Cart: Did anything change, can I edit safely, and what happens next?
- Checkout: Can I pay, understand delivery, recover errors, and trust data handling?

## Finding anatomy

Every finding needs observed evidence, customer question, behavioral mechanism, funnel stage, reach, confidence, recommendation, acceptance criterion, and measurement plan.

## Priority model

- Impact: expected size of behavior improvement
- Reach: traffic or revenue exposed
- Confidence: strength and relevance of evidence
- Effort: design, engineering, content, operations, and QA cost
- Reversibility: cost and risk of undoing the change

Do not collapse a legal, accessibility, or measurement defect into a low score merely because direct revenue impact is hard to quantify.
