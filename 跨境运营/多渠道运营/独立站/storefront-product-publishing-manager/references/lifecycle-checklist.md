# Product Lifecycle Checklist

## Publish or schedule preflight

- Correct store, environment, product ID, variants, market, language, channel, and effective time
- Approved title, description, media, options, SKU, barcode, taxonomy, vendor, and attributes
- Verified price, compare-at price, tax behavior, cost, currency, discount compatibility, and legal pricing display
- Inventory source, available quantity, oversell policy, safety stock, backorder promise, and replenishment ownership
- Weight, dimensions, customs data, dangerous-goods flags, shipping profile, fulfillment location, and delivery promise
- Product claims, warnings, certifications, age restrictions, privacy implications, and market-specific legal text
- Variant selection, size or fit help, accessibility, alt text, translation, currency, and mobile behavior
- Collection membership, navigation, onsite search, recommendations, bundles, subscriptions, and related products
- URL handle, canonical, structured data, sitemap, internal links, preview cards, and tracking
- Sales-channel eligibility, feed attributes, marketplace policy, campaign creative, email, affiliate, and support readiness
- Test order or preview evidence and named approval owner

## Hide, retire, archive, or delete preflight

- Reason, requested duration, affected variants, markets, channels, and restoration condition
- Open orders, returns, warranties, subscriptions, bundles, kits, replacement parts, and customer support obligations
- Active ads, email links, affiliates, social posts, QR codes, feeds, wishlists, carts, saved links, and external backlinks
- Inventory disposition, purchase orders, warehouse stock, replenishment, and finance treatment
- Replacement product, collection alternative, support page, or intentional gone state
- URL traffic, revenue, backlinks, search demand, and historical analytics preservation
- Redirect ownership, destination relevance, canonical behavior, sitemap removal, and cache propagation
- Data retention, audit, legal hold, and recovery capability
- Rollback owner, restoration steps, and monitoring window

## Blocking conditions

- Product identity, store, market, action, or authorization is ambiguous
- Required price, inventory, fulfillment, compliance, or customer claim is unverified
- A live dependency would break without a coordinated change
- The requested action is destructive and recovery or retention has not been evaluated
